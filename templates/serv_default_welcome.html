Next Steps on Your Serv Adventure

  • Create Your First Route: Define a Route subclass (e.g., in routes.py). Example: class MyRoute(Route): async def handle_get(self, req): return TextResponse("Hello, Serv!")
  • Configure Your App: Create or modify serv_config.yaml for site info, plugins, and middleware.
  • Register Routes/Plugins: Add your routes via plugins in serv_config.yaml or directly in your app setup.
  • Explore Plugins: Enhance functionality with custom or community Serv plugins.
  • Run Your App: Use python -m serv. Add --reload for development.
  • Consult the Docs: For advanced features, visit getserv.ing/docs (Coming Soon!).
